Key Assumptions

Revenue Assumptions

  • Each pub runs 1 competition per season
  • Average 10 teams per pub competition
  • $1,000 buy-in per team (jackpot contribution)
  • Platform retains 10% commission upfront
  • 90% of jackpot held in 4% p.a. term deposit
  • Advertising ramps from $2K → $150K Y1–Y4
  • Advertising priced at CPM + sponsorship packages

Cost Assumptions

  • Netlify Pro ($19/mo) scales to Enterprise at Y4
  • Supabase Pro ($25/mo) upgrades to Team ($599/mo) at Y3
  • Claude AI API costs ~$0.05 per bet verification cycle
  • Payment processing at 1.75% of jackpot collections
  • No founding-team salary in Y1 (sweat equity)
  • Part-time hire Y2; full-time team from Y3 onward
  • Legal costs include gambling compliance & KYC

Risk Factors

  • Pub partner acquisition speed may vary
  • Regulatory changes to prize competitions
  • Payment processor availability for prize pools
  • Advertising revenue is estimated — not contracted
  • Term deposit rate may fluctuate with RBA cash rate
  • Team drop-out rate not modelled (assumed negligible)
  • Competition from alternative pub entertainment
